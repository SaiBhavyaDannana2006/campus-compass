A Day in the Life of a Resident

Living at Amrita is structured, energetic, and highly rhythmic. Here is how your standard weekdays unfold.

1

07:30 AM — 09:00 AM

Breakfast & Daily Prep

Grab hot vegetarian breakfast staples (Idli, Dosa, Poha) with quick service. Check your slot timetable and head to classrooms.

2

09:00 AM — 05:00 PM

Classroom & Lab Sprints

Academic sessions span 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (lunch break 12:00 PM — 1:00 PM). Attendance gates close on the dot. Stay active during system lab sessions.

3

Gym & Snacks Time

Gym, Sports & Snacks

Gym Timings: Morning: 06:00 AM — 08:30 AM
Evening: 05:00 PM — 08:00 PM
Tea & Snacks: 05:15 PM — 06:30 PM
Hit fitness rooms, outdoor courts, or enjoy hot high-tea snacks.

4

08:30 PM Lockout

Hostel Curfew & Discipline

All students must enter hostels before 8:30 PM curfew. Late infractions are reported to the discipline committee and penalized with a 2,000 regulatory fine.

The Honest Infrastructure Review

No promotional literature. Here is the absolute, unfiltered report of services on the ground, written directly by resident seniors.

Mess Meals (100% Pure Vegetarian)

Nutritious and balanced

Hygienic, 100% vegetarian meals. Sambar, sabzi, rotis, and weekly special paneer are standard staples.

Official Mess Policy Reminder

NOTICE: Mess Hall Policy

Dear Students,

Carrying food from the mess hall to rooms is strictly prohibited with immediate effect. Please follow regulations.

Senior Out:

Paneer night (Wednesdays) is packed. Get in line 10 minutes early!

Cafeteria Alternatives

Snacks, Shakes & Juices

Head to the canteen for juices, shakes, noodles, parottas, sandwiches, and bakery snacks at student rates.

Senior Out:

Grab a vegetable puff and hot chai during late evening study sessions.

Campus & Hostel Wi-Fi

Standardized Internet Ports

Individual credentials are provided during onboarding, with reliable speed across academic blocks. Signal strength varies near specific thick-walled resident wings.

Senior Out:

Campus proxy blocks gaming servers. Pull heavy compiler assets and code libraries during peak afternoon hours in lab blocks.

Laundry & Support Outlets

Regular washing routines

Washing service bags are collected twice weekly per hostel wing by outsource partners. Clean clothes are returned dried and folded.

Senior Out:

Write your roll number on clothes using permanent marker to avoid mixed-up returns.

Official Campus Security Circular
🙏 Om Namah Shivaya 🙏
Regarding bringing/using Unauthorized Electrical Appliances in Campus

Dear Students,

Bringing electrical appliances exceeding 300 watts is strictly prohibited. Security authorities will permanently confiscate any unauthorized items. Confiscated appliances will not be returned.

Amaravathi campus
Regards, CSO
Heavy-Duty Extension Cord (Anchor or Belkin) Power sockets are sometimes distant from study desks. Pack a 3-4 meter extension cord for dual devices.
Sturdy Umbrella (3-Fold, Heavy-Duty) Amaravati gets heavy monsoon showers during July/August. Carry a dependable umbrella to prevent getting drenched during block transitions.
Comfortable Walking/Running Shoes With spacious distances between hostels, mess halls, and engineering labs, you will hit 7,000+ steps daily. Bring supportive trainers.
Personal Locker Padlocks & Duplicates Keys Bring robust metal padlocks (like Godrej) with duplicate keys to lock your laptop cabinets securely.
Low-Wattage Electric Kettle (strictly under 300 Watts!) Must be strictly under 300W. High-power kettles, inductions, or heaters are prohibited and permanently confiscated.
