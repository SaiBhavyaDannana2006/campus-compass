Hostel Logistics & Rules

Your block gets bi-weekly slots. Bring your stamped mesh laundry bag (provided at room allotment). Wash and dry cycles are fully covered in hostel fees. Ironing carries nominal fees at campus stores.

Local Area Transport

Catch APSRTC buses from Kunchanapalli Main Road Junction (every 15-25 mins) to PNBS Vijayawada or Guntur Central. Alternatively, book shared autos or Ola/Uber directly to the main gate.

Closest hubs: Vijayawada Railway Junction (BZA) is 14 km (30 mins), Guntur Junction (GNT) is 25 km. Vijayawada International Airport (VGA) is 32 km. Share taxi bookings are coordinated via Warden boards before term breaks.

Campus Holiday & Outpass Schedules

Yes. Hostels, Wi-Fi, and dining remain active during minor state/national holidays. Food schedules might be adjusted slightly. Room vacation is required only during the summer maintenance block (June/July).

Apply on CMS 24 hours prior with parental verification to get Warden approval, then swipe physical student ID card at the gate.

1. Submit Request: Apply on CMS at least 24 hours in advance with exact timings.

2. Parental Verification: Guardians must approve the request via official call, SMS, or app portal.

3. Warden Approval: Hostel Resident Warden will electronically approve the verified outpass.

4. ID Swipe: Swipe your physical student ID card at the perimeter gate upon leaving and returning.

💡 Helpful Tips:
  • Timelines: Apply 24h ahead. Same-day applications are for emergencies only.
  • Quota: Limit of 2-3 weekend passes per month.
