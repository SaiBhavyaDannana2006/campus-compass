Academic Life & Regulations

Amrita is known for high-standard academic systems. Here is exactly how classes run, grades are formulated, and how to avoid the dreaded attendance penalty.

Daily Timing 09:00 AM — 05:00 PM

The Absolute Law: Strict 75% Attendance Mandate

Amrita University enforces a non-negotiable 75% minimum attendance per course. Failing to meet this metric results in an immediate 'FA' (Shortage of Attendance) grade. Registering an 'FA' means you are disqualified from writing the End-Sem, and must pay remediation fees to repeat the course in winter/summer fast-track schedules.

    Treat P1 Exams Like Gold Dust P1 syllabus is brief. Earning 13+/15 in P1 is much easier than in P2. Solid P1 scores ease mid-term pressure.
