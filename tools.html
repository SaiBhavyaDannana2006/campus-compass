Hostel Wi-Fi Configuration Guideline for Linux/Windows Users

Getting your laptop online in the hostel requires specific parameter adjustments during authentication. Standard "plug-and-play" settings won't establish connection. Follow these properties:

SSID: Amrita-WiFi
Security Type: WPA2-Enterprise (EAP)
EAP Authentication Method: PEAP
Phase-2 Authentication: MSCHAPv2
CA Certificate: Do Not Validate / No Cert
Identity: Your Student Roll Number (e.g. AM.EN.U4CSE26001)
Password: [Default pin distributed in onboarding files]

Note: If connection errors persist on your specific laptop models, do not panic. Carry your system to the Ground Floor IT Support desk located in academic Block-1. The system administrators will register your MAC addresses manually and authorize access.
